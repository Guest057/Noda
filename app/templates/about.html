About us

Our platform offers an innovative way to interact with videos, taking the viewing experience to a new level. We have created a system that allows users to fully utilize video content through convenient analysis tools. With our service, not only can you access subtitles, but you also gain a deeper understanding of the material.
We can provide you with:

  • Getting subtitles from video. Even if there are no subtitles on YouTube, we can create them for you using artificial intelligence.
  • We will help you with questions about the video. Our neural network will generate an answer based on the material in the text.
  • Too lazy to write questions from the text? Try permanent text scanning for all possible questions!

Service Information

Parameters Description
Speed The speed of video analysis depends on its size. The larger the video, the longer it will take to process.
Integration YouTube does not allow direct subtitles from videos and blocks such activity. Therefore, subtitles are received with a certain delay.
Accessibility The interface is basic and clear. Every user will be able to understand it (except the blind).
Language Our platform supports most languages, but for best accuracy it is better to use English.
Dependence It should be understood that the speed and quality of our service depends on the capacity of our servers. In the initial stages, our forces are limited.
